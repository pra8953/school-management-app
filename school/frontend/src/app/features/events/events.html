Independence Day Celebration

Date: 15 August 2025
Event Type: National Event
Location: School Ground

Event Details:
Join us for a day of patriotic celebrations, including flag hoisting, cultural performances, and a special address by the principal. We encourage all students and parents to participate in this significant event to honor our nation's independence. The event will commence at 9:00 AM. Please ensure your presence by 8:30 AM for the flag hoisting ceremony.
